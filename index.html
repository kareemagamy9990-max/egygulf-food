About Us

Pioneer in
Snacks & Confectionery
Since 2004

Egy Gulf Company was established in 2004 under the name Delta Foods Industries, later becoming the Egyptian-Gulf Company. A pioneer in the automatic production of snacks and dry confectionery, with branches throughout Egypt and an international presence in 30+ countries.

Distinguished by introducing the first healthy oven-roasted snacks in Egypt, Egy Gulf employs the latest methods and advanced techniques ensuring the highest quality and compliance with global safety standards.
